### Our Roadmap is Guided by You

The future of MijnUI is shaped by your input. We prioritize the features and improvements that matter most to you. Vote for the features you want to see next by upvoting issues on GitHub.

### Stay Updated

To see our progress, visit the [MijnUI Project Board on GitHub](https://github.com/orgs/mijn-ui/projects/1). You'll find:

- **What’s Coming**: Upcoming features.
- **Currently Being Worked On**: Features in development.
- **What’s Done**: Completed features.
